Nica DAO

Since 18 April 2018, Nicaraguan citizens have protested the Ortega government, initially due to an attempt by the government to thieve upon its people by increasing taxes and slashing benefits. The protests have continued and hundreds of protestors and activists have been imprisoned.

2021 is a national election year in Nicaragua, however, the Ortega-Murillo regime have detained the oposition candidates. In December 2020, a law was passed to facilitate the imprisonment of any opposition on the basis of possibly stirring social unrest. Law 1055 ultimately makes any opposition to the ruling party a terrorist act. Since late spring 2021, the police have arrested opposition candidates and high profile activists using Law 1055 as pretense for detainment. Inspired by the concepts underlying decentralized autonomous organization, Nica DAO speculates on a future Nicaragua, free of the current dictatorship and the use of blockchain to support the families of political prisoners.
